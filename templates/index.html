Our Mission

Bridging the healthcare gap in rural communities

At AFYAMKONONI, our mission is to bridge the healthcare gap in rural Mombasa, ensuring that every woman has access to quality prenatal and postnatal care. We believe that technology can be a powerful tool in saving lives and promoting well-being.

We are committed to working with local healthcare providers and communities to make a real difference.
